Exploring Modern Data Architecture

MODACamp is an unconference where early adapters of data technologies exchange ideas. With the rapid change occurring in the industry, we need a place we can meet to share our experiences, challenges and solutions. At MODACamp, you are encouraged you to share your thoughts in several open discussions, as we strive for the advancement of Generative AI for Data. End users, developers, IT professionals and vendors are all encouraged to participate. We hope you can join us. And let us know if you'd like to put on a MODACamp in your city or region:
